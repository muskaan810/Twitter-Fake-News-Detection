What is VeBRANDS?

VeBRANDS is a comprehensive brand management software that helps you keep track of social media mentions, analyze engagement, and manage your brand's online narrative. With VeBRANDS, you can effortlessly monitor tweets about your brand, analyze sentiment, detect fake news, and make informed marketing decisions.

Why VeBRANDS?

First, VeBRANDS is a powerful, user-friendly platform designed to provide end-to-end social media management. Whether you need to track mentions, analyze engagement, or compare your brand's performance with competitors, VeBRANDS has you covered.

Second, our AI-ML-based fake news detection module ensures you can identify and address false information quickly. This feature helps maintain your brand's reputation and allows for prompt engagement with genuine users.

Lastly, VeBRANDS offers comprehensive analytics, including sentiment analysis, engagement metrics, and geographical data. This data empowers brand managers to refine their marketing strategies and stay ahead of the competition. VeBRANDS also makes influencer marketing easier by identifying top positive influencers and flagging negative propagators for quick action.

Characteristics of Fake News:

Detecting fake news can be complex and time-consuming. That’s why our platform offers an exclusive feature that automatically identifies key characteristics of fake news with advanced algorithms. By leveraging real-time data analysis, we help you spot:

  • Irrelevant Hashtags: Our system detects hashtags that are not related to the content, which are often used to artificially boost visibility and engagement.
  • Promotional Language: We analyze for excessive punctuation, emojis, and urgency that might indicate the tweet is trying to sell something rather than provide genuine information.
  • Offensive Language: Our platform identifies provocative or offensive language that could be used to attract attention and stir controversy.
  • Misleading Information: We automatically flag tweets that spread false or misleading information, ensuring you receive accurate and reliable content.
  • Repetitive Content: Repeated messages are flagged as potential spam, helping to maintain the integrity of your feed by filtering out artificially amplified content.
